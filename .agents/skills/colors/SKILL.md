# Color System

Only the following core colors are permitted across all Kylrix user interfaces. Do not introduce default third-party brand colors (like Telegram blue).

## Primary Colors

1. **Kylrix (Core)**: `#6366F1` (Indigo)
2. **Kylrix Connect**: `#F59E0B` (Amber)
3. **Kylrix Vault**: `#10B981` (Emerald)
4. **Kylrix Note**: `#EC4899` (Pink)
5. **Kylrix Flow**: `#A855F7` (Purple)

## Neutrals & Accents

- **Base Backgrounds**: `#161412`
- **Internal Cards**: `#0B0A09`
- **Borders & Dividers**: `white/5` or `border-[#34322F]`
- **Text & Status**: Sentence case in whites, muted ashes, and deep pitch blacks.
